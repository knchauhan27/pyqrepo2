About RepoMed

Welcome to RepoMed – Your Digital PYQ Repository

What We Offer

RepoMed is a comprehensive digital repository hosting previous year questions (PYQs) from medical university exams and preliminary examinations. We believe in making quality study materials accessible to all medical students.

Our Mission

To empower medical students by providing organized, easily searchable collections of previous year examination questions. Our goal is to help students prepare effectively and efficiently for their medical board exams.

What's Included

  • 📚 Multiple Subjects: Anatomy, Physiology, Biochemistry, Pathology, Pharmacology, and more
  • 🎯 Organized Questions: Questions organized by subject and examination year
  • 🔍 Smart Search: Easily find questions by topic or keyword
  • 📥 Quick Download: Export questions as PDF for offline studying
  • 🔐 Secure Access: Sign in to personalize your learning experience

About Our Platform

Built by Brainspire in collaboration with GMERS Gotri & BMC, RepoMed serves the medical education community with a modern, mobile-friendly interface. Whether you're preparing for your semester exams or board exams, we've got you covered.

For questions or suggestions, reach out to us on GitHub or contact us directly.
